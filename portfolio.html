What I've Been Working On

Clean Energy Social

Concepted and founded Clean Energy Social, the clean energy professional network, in 2020. Current and aspiring clean energy professionals can research clean energy employers, apply for jobs, discover training opportunities, and network with other clean energy professionals.

Solar Energy International

Joined the Board of Directors of Solar Energy International in 2020. Solar Energy International has provided industry-leading technical training and expertise to over 70,000 students and alumni, with a goal of training 272,500 students and alumni by 2025.

Solar Marketing Experts

Concepted and founded Solar Marketing Experts, a solar customer acquisition marketing agency, in 2016. Our proven digital advertising model has generated 1,500,000+ residential solar leads over the past four years, worth an estimated $300,000,000 in contracted revenue.

Clean Energy Collective

Joined Clean Energy Collective as a Co-Owner and Vice President of Marketing in 2013. Over two years, I helped this startup into its high growth stage. In 2014, our marketing efforts resulted in significant sales growth and a #194 ranking on the Inc. 5000 list of fastest growing private companies.

SolarReviews

Concepted and founded SolarReviews, the industry's leading consumer reviews website, in 2012. Developed the company into the most successful, well respected customer acquisition websites, with hundreds of solar installer partners, 20,000+ consumer reviews, and over 1,000,000 unique visitors annually.

CRES

Joined the Board of Directors of Colorado Renewable Energy Society in 2009. Colorado Renewable Energy Society drives environmental, social and economic benefits for Colorado through clean energy education, policy advocacy, community engagement, and promoting the adoption of renewable energy technologies.
